Instructions

At the the top of the screen is a navigation bar where you will be able to navigate the website. In the navigation bar you can simply hover over the drop down menus in order to expand the list. Once you find a link that interests you you can click on it to navigate to that page. The Study button in the navigation bar leads you to a page that displays random trivia question and answers for practice. The Modes button on the navigation bar leads you to a page displaying six different quiz topics that you can choose to do and two types of quiz choices. ThePlay Quiz button on the navigation bar leads you to a the Trivia Quiz, before starting you will have two different test options to choose from.
